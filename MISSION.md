# Mission

BrandTrace's mission is to build the world's most trusted consumer ownership database.

## Long-Term Vision

Consumers should be able to understand the ownership relationships behind the products they buy. BrandTrace will support this by connecting products, brands, parent companies, acquisition history, corporate structures, and documented sources in a clear and verifiable way.

## Commitments

BrandTrace commits to:

- Consumer transparency without political direction.
- Source-based ownership documentation.
- Clear separation between verified information and unverified submissions.
- Community participation with review safeguards.
- Long-term data integrity.
- Neutral language and careful presentation.

## Success Standard

BrandTrace succeeds when consumers, researchers, journalists, and businesses can rely on the platform as a careful, well-sourced, and neutral reference for consumer brand ownership information.
