# Code of Conduct

BrandTrace is committed to a respectful, neutral, and evidence-focused community.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully.
- Use neutral language.
- Support factual claims with sources whenever possible.
- Separate facts from opinions.
- Welcome good-faith corrections.
- Focus on transparency and consumer information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks.
- Unsupported accusations presented as fact.
- Political campaigning through project channels.
- Coordinated attacks on companies, contributors, or communities.
- Misrepresentation of unverified submissions as verified information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation when conduct undermines trust, neutrality, accuracy, or community safety.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the project maintainers. Reports should include relevant context, links, and examples where possible.
